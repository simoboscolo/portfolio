simone boscolo

Graphic, web, illustration.

I started graphic design when i was 12. I had an online forum with a couple of friends about Marvel Comics and mangas and i wanted it to look pretty. I never realized how much i liked to create those stupid headers and pictures until i was 14 and i decided to study graphic design, communication, photography and cinema at the Italian State Institute of Arts.

After my studies i went to London to study English and then to Amsterdam. I belive the incredible mix of cultures I've met during my first period here influenced, changed and improved me even more then i could possibly say. Feel free to ask me for anything, I'm always open to new experiences!

Cheers!
